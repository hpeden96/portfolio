Byte Jam 2020

Pixel President

Byte Jam is a yearly coding competition where groups have a 3 day period to creat some type of program or website with a theme.
The theme this year was "Patriotism" so our group decided to make a game call Pixel President. The premise of the game is to make
choice as a candidate to win the election. Every choice you make will determine if you become the President at the end of the campaign!
